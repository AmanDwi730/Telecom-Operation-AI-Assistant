Telecom Alarm Playbook

Cell Down:
- Check site power, backhaul, and radio unit health.
- Verify whether the fault is isolated to one sector or the entire site.
- Review recent configuration changes and alarm correlation windows.
- Recommended action: restore power, validate sync, and re-run cell availability checks.

SCTP / Signaling Failure:
- Validate MME / AMF reachability and transport path continuity.
- Check firewall rules, IP route stability, and interface utilization.
- Recommended action: inspect link state, packet loss, and retransmission counters.

High PRB Utilization:
- Correlate with busy hour traffic and interference.
- Check scheduler configuration, power headroom, and user distribution.
- Recommended action: optimize load balancing and capacity planning.
