Telecom RCA and Recovery Notes

AMF Registration Failure:
- Possible causes: authentication mismatch, control-plane congestion, DNS resolution issues, stale subscriber context.
- Steps: verify AMF reachability, inspect N2/NAS traces, validate subscriber profile, and check UE mobility history.

VoLTE Call Drop:
- Possible causes: SIP timeout, IMS SBC issue, transport jitter, radio quality degradation.
- Steps: check SIP traces, verify IMS registration, review RTP packet loss, and inspect handover events.

Fiber Cut:
- Possible causes: physical cable damage, construction work, weather impact, splice failure.
- Steps: reroute traffic to backup path, dispatch field team, and verify attenuation once repaired.
