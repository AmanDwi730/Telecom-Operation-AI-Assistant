Telecom KPI Cheat Sheet

- Registration success rate: should remain stable and near the operational baseline.
- Session setup time: spikes often indicate signaling congestion or policy control delays.
- Packet loss: strong indicator of transport degradation, congestion, or optical issues.
- Latency: may increase during backhaul congestion, routing loops, or remote transport impairment.
- PRB utilization: sustained high values indicate radio capacity pressure.
- Call drop rate: inspect radio quality, handover success, and IMS path stability.
